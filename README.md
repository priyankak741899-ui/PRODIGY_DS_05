# PRODIGY_DS_04 - Task 04

## Project
Road Accident Data Analysis and Visualization

## Objective
To analyze and visualize accident patterns related to road conditions, weather conditions, and time of day, and identify accident hotspots and contributing factors.

## Dataset
US Accidents Dataset (2016 - 2023)

## Steps
- Data loading and preprocessing
- Analysis of weather conditions
- Analysis of road conditions
- Analysis of time of day
- Visualization of accident hotspots
- Analysis of contributing factors

## Result
The analysis identified accident patterns based on weather, road conditions, and time of day. Visualizations highlighted accident hotspots and major contributing factors affecting road safety.

## Tools Used
Python, Pandas, Matplotlib, Seaborn
